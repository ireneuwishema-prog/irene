DISCOVER SAFETY CENTER

Discord is the communications platform that enables you to build meaningful
connections around the joy of playing games through voice, video and text features.
We’re deeply committed to creating positive experiences for our users. In the Discord Safety Center,
you can find the resources you need - whether you’re a teen, parent, educator, or a long-time Discord user.
